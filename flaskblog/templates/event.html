EVENTS

C.U.B.E VIT successfully managed to organise 7 cubing events and released 2 video series. We have planned to conduct a number of events in order to empower our goals and provide the best we can to the cubing community!

OUR EVENTS

TACTICAL 2x2

Tactical 2x2 is an online 1 vs 1 battle between 4 of India's leading 2x2 speedcubers

PERPLEXED PYRAMINX

Perplexed Pyraminx is an online 1 vs 1 battle between 4 of India's leading pyraminx solvers

FANTASTIC 4x4

Fantastic 4x4 is an online 1 vs 1 battle between 4 of India's leading 4x4 speedcubers

THRILLING 3X3

Thrilling 3x3 is an online 1 vs 1 battle between 4 of India's leading 3x3 speedcubers

OVERPOWERED 3x3 OH

Over Powered 3x3 OH is an online 1 vs 1 battle between 4 of India's leading 3x3 OH speedcubers

CUBE UNLEASHED 1.0

Cube Unleashed 1.0 is a national level online cubing competitions consisting of 4 WCA events!

RIVIERA CUBE OPEN

Riviera Cube Open is an intra VIT cubing competition conducted by CUBE VIT during Riviera.
